Guide 06 · Go-to-Market

The operator's playbook.
Channels, positioning, metrics.

Product value proposition, market context, competitive positioning, channel strategy by phase, target metrics, and the 12-month execution roadmap.

Executive Summary

Key Lime is an AI-powered real estate platform that enables buyers and sellers to transact without traditional agents, charging 1% at closing vs. the industry standard 5.44%. Platform features: AI negotiation, automated document generation, a DoorDash-style chaperone network ($20 per walkthrough), and a unified transaction hub. Initial market: Tampa Bay, FL. On a $430K median-priced Tampa home, the seller keeps ~$19,100 more than under the traditional commission model.

01 · Product Value Proposition

AI-powered full service. Not a discount broker. Not FSBO.

Key Lime unbundles the five things a traditional agent bundles — and delivers each one better, separately, and without the conflict of interest.

05 · Channel Strategy

Three tiers, sequenced by phase.

Foundation channels first — they compound. Growth channels layer on once the first 100 transactions exist as proof. Scale channels amplify proven winners.

Tier 01 · Foundation
Compounding, SEO-led
Months 1–3

SEO Content Engine

Non-negotiable. 50 articles in 90 days, 200+ by month 6. Content clusters: "Sell home without agent Tampa," commission alternatives, AI real estate platform reviews, comparison pages. Playbook: Opendoor's pain-point SEO + Clever's 8,000-article moat.

Google Search PPC

Opendoor's single-landing-page model. Priority keywords: "sell my house Tampa," "sell home without agent," "low commission real estate FL." Competitor bidding on Houzeo, Clever, Redfin terms with comparison messaging.

Savings Calculator

The single most important conversion tool. Shows exact dollar savings vs. traditional agent, Redfin, Houzeo, FSBO. Placed on homepage hero, every blog post CTA, every landing page. Shareable results page for social proof.

Email Drip

Calculator users → education series → listing conversion. Triggered emails on price drops, new listings, transaction status. Weekly Tampa Bay market newsletter positions Key Lime as the Sage of the market.

Tier 02 · Growth
Paid + owned audience
Months 3–6

Meta (Facebook + Instagram)

Seller targeting on life-event signals + Tampa Bay radius. Carousel ads, video testimonials, savings comparison static. Chaperone recruitment targeting Tampa gig workers. Retargeting calculator users who didn't sign up.

YouTube

Weekly Tampa market explainer. "How AI handles your negotiation" demo. Customer savings stories with specific dollar amounts. Neighborhood tours targeting out-of-state relocators. Format: 5–10 min educational, not polished commercial.

Podcast Advertising

Underexploited by competitors — no major competitor is a heavy podcast advertiser. Priority: BiggerPockets, Money Guy, Dave Ramsey, local Tampa shows, Side Hustle Nation (chaperone recruitment). Host-read with calculator CTA.

Comparison Landing Pages

vs. Redfin, Zillow, Houzeo, Clever. Each with a live side-by-side fee calculator. These double as SEO pages for branded-competitor search and as bottom-funnel conversion pages.

Tier 03 · Scale
Amplify the winners
Months 6–12

Streaming / OTT Video

Hulu, Peacock, YouTube TV — cost-efficient and targetable. 30-second spots with real Tampa homeowners and specific savings amounts. Targeting: Tampa Bay DMA, homeowner demographics, $80K+ HHI.

TikTok + Instagram Reels

"Commission reveals," AI negotiation demos, chaperone day-in-the-life. Organic first → amplify winners with paid. This is where Gen Z and young millennial awareness lives.

PR & Media

Tampa Bay Times, HousingWire, Inman, Real Trends. Angle: commission disruption + AI replacing agents + local success stories. Publish data-driven studies for pickup — Clever got cited by NYT and WSJ doing this.

Local Partnerships

Tampa Bay REIA (investor segment), relocation companies (out-of-state referrals), title/mortgage/lender referral relationships. Every local partner is a trust multiplier.
